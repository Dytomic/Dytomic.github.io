The Studio

About Dytomic

Independent game development studio formerly based in Montreal, Quebec.

Dytomic was founded to build Pirate Raids Online — a PC MMORPG drawing from the classic Tales of Pirates / Pirate King Online (PKO) engine heritage, reimagined with modern design expectations. The studio operated from 2021 through early 2024 before ceasing operations.

All repositories are now public and archived. The code, art direction, and game design of Pirate Raids Online remain browsable as an open technical record.

Lore

Defend Ascaron from the New World Order

A tyrant regime known as The Navy seeks to burn, pillage, and capture all within reach of their expanding empire.

Their conquest stretches outwards into the seas and islands of Ascaron, wreaking havoc and threatening all who stand in the way of their new world order. A group of rebels and freedom fighters have taken up arms against the merciless tyrants — but to no avail.

However, there is still hope. As ancient prophecies once foretold, the unification of the Soulstones shall fulfill a sacred primordial ritual and give rise to the divine summoning of Elefrihet — the almighty god of justice and supreme patron of Ascaron. In a collective effort to end The Navy's conquest, the rebellion established The Pirate Coalition: an alliance between the pirate tribes of men seeking to reunite the Demigods, now lost to time and isolation, to fulfill the ancient prophecy and save humanity from annihilation.

Playable Races

The Four Demigod Tribes of Ascaron

Over a thousand years ago, Elefrihet, the god of justice and patron of Ascaron, created a race of Demigods to defend humanity against the forces of Torantika, goddess of death.

Dwarves

Endowed with dominion and guardianship over the mountains of Ascaron. Their defensive tactics and expertise in fortifying structures pose a great threat to Navy invasions.

Triton

Inhabitants and guardians of the seas, ensuring dark evil shall never again plague the waters of Ascaron. Expert shipbuilders and masters of the sea with nautical prowess that intimidates even the highest ranked Navy captains.

Runeborne

Wardens of the tundras and forests of Ascaron. Their ferocity and ruthlessness in battle threatens the safety and security of Navy encampments throughout the land.

Amazonians

Equipped with predatory instincts that enhance their agility and swiftness, the Amazonian Demigods are silent killers feared by all — lethal protectors of the jungle.

Gameplay

Explore a Dynamic World on Both Land and Sea

Whether on land or sea, the world of Ascaron is abundant with wildlife and rich in resources.

Master the pioneered skills of the Demigods and gather a wide range of materials to improve weapons, equipment, ships, and structures to strengthen the might of The Pirate Coalition. Set sail on your very own ship and travel to islands and continents where you can seek prized goods and ancient treasures, raid Navy encampments, or test your mettle with fellow pirates.

PvE

The tyrannical actions of The Navy have tainted Ascaron with dark forces and energies not seen for thousands of years. Deadly dungeons from the days of old are resurfacing, and mythical monsters are laying waste to island inhabitants.

PvP

The Pirate Coalition welcomes its members to engage in competition. Hone your skills and prove your might among fellow pirates in risk-based PvP arenas and territories to compete for glory and prized rewards.

Combat System

Master Your Skills in a Unique Classless Combat System

The Pirate Coalition does not discriminate over the style of combat you wish to use — so long as it brings destruction to the Navy.

Master up to 10 weapon builds featuring unique combat styles and ability trees. For fighters seeking a more versatile experience, combine 1-handed weapons with off-hand gear or other weapon types to create hybrid combat builds.

Melee

Sharpen your skills in close-range combat and become a fierce warrior in the Pirate Coalition.

Ranged

Hone your skills in ranged combat and become an apex predator in the Pirate Coalition.

Magic

Master various disciplines of magic combat and become a sacred acolyte in the Pirate Coalition.

Naval Combat

Sail to New Horizons

The Demigods have perfected seafaring for thousands of years, with each tribe having developed their own take on ship mastery.

Your journey at sea begins once you prove yourself capable of constructing your own ship that appropriately honours the Demigods of your tribe. Once at sea, become a ship master by improving your skills in combat, exploration, fishing, and constructing ship upgrades.

Game Systems

Additional Features

Pets

Track down and tame wondrous creatures of Ascaron. Useful as helpers on your journey or flaunted by your side as adornments.

Mounts

Bond or break beasts at the Amberock Stables. Travel far and wide on the lands of Ascaron to serve the Pirate Coalition more efficiently.

Life Skills

Master the ancient crafts of the Demigods. Supply the Coalition's demand for weapons, equipment, and resources across up to 10 unique life skills.

Player-Driven Economy

Free trade among pirates with supply and demand dictated by the needs and wants of the Pirate Coalition — contrary to the will of the Navy.

In-Game Trading

Whether on the road or at the famous Pirate Exchange, trade or exchange goods with fellow pirates and enter trade auctions.

Guild System

Old-school MMORPG guild mechanics — guild wars, territory control, and coordinated raid encounters against the Navy.

Open Source Research

Tales of Pirates and PKO Development Archives

Pirate Raids Online was built on deep research into the Tales of Pirates (TOP) and Pirate King Online (PKO) engine. Those findings are preserved here as open-source monorepo mirrors.

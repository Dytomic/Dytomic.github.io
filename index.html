The Studio

About Dytomic

Independent game development studio formerly based in Montreal, Quebec.

Dytomic was founded to build Pirate Raids Online — a PC MMORPG drawing from the classic Tales of Pirates and Pirate King Online (PKO) engine heritage, reimagined with modern design expectations. The studio operated from 2021 through early 2024 before ceasing operations.

All repositories are now public and archived. The code, art direction, and game design of Pirate Raids Online remain browsable as an open technical record.

Lore

Defend Ascaron from the New World Order

A tyrant regime known as The Navy seeks to burn, pillage, and capture all within reach of their expanding empire.

Their conquest stretches outwards into the seas and islands of Ascaron, wreaking havoc and threatening all who stand in the way of their new world order. A group of rebels and freedom fighters have taken up arms against the merciless tyrants — but to no avail.

However, there is still hope. As ancient prophecies once foretold, the unification of the Soulstones shall fulfill a sacred primordial ritual and give rise to the divine summoning of Elefrihet — the almighty god of justice and supreme patron of Ascaron. In a collective effort to end The Navy's conquest, the rebellion established The Pirate Coalition: an alliance between the pirate tribes seeking to reunite the Demigods, now lost to time and isolation, to fulfill the ancient prophecy and save humanity from annihilation.

Playable Races

The Four Demigod Tribes of Ascaron

Over a thousand years ago, Elefrihet created a race of Demigods to defend humanity against the forces of Torantika, goddess of death.

Dwarves

Guardians of the mountains. Their defensive tactics and expertise in fortifying structures pose a great threat to Navy invasions.

Triton

Masters of the sea and expert shipbuilders. Their nautical prowess intimidates even the highest ranked Navy captains.

Runeborne

Wardens of the tundras and forests. Their ferocity and ruthlessness in battle threatens Navy encampments throughout the land.

Amazonians

Equipped with predatory instincts, enhanced agility, and swiftness. Silent killers — lethal protectors of the jungle.

Gameplay

Explore a Dynamic World on Both Land and Sea

Whether on land or sea, the world of Ascaron is abundant with wildlife and rich in resources.

Master the pioneered skills of the Demigods and gather materials to improve weapons, equipment, ships, and structures. Set sail on your very own ship to islands and continents where you can seek prized goods, raid Navy encampments, or test your mettle with fellow pirates.

PvE

The tyrannical actions of The Navy have tainted Ascaron with dark forces not seen for thousands of years. Deadly dungeons from the days of old are resurfacing, and mythical monsters are laying waste to island inhabitants.

PvP

The Pirate Coalition welcomes its members to compete in risk-based PvP arenas and territories. Hone your skills and prove your might among fellow pirates to claim glory and prized rewards.

Combat System

A Unique Classless Combat System

The Pirate Coalition does not discriminate over the style of combat you wish to use — so long as it brings destruction to the Navy.

Master up to 10 weapon builds featuring unique combat styles and ability trees. Combine 1-handed weapons with off-hand gear or other weapon types to create hybrid builds that best suit your playstyle.

Melee

Sharpen your skills in close-range combat and become a fierce warrior in the Pirate Coalition.

Ranged

Hone your skills in ranged combat and become an apex predator in the Pirate Coalition.

Magic

Master various disciplines of magic combat and become a sacred acolyte in the Pirate Coalition.

Game Systems

Features

Ships

Each Demigod tribe has perfected seafaring for thousands of years. Build your own ship honouring your tribe and master combat, exploration, fishing, and ship upgrades at sea.

Pets & Mounts

Tame wondrous creatures of Ascaron as companions or adornments. Bond or break beasts at the Amberock Stables to travel the lands more efficiently.

Life Skills

Master the ancient crafts of the Demigods across up to 10 unique life skills and supply the Coalition's demand for weapons, equipment, and resources.

Player Economy

Free trade among pirates with supply and demand dictated by the needs of the Pirate Coalition — contrary to the will of the Navy.

Trading System

Trade or exchange goods with fellow pirates on the road or at the famous Pirate Exchange. Enter trade auctions and build your fortune.

Guild System

Old-school MMORPG guild mechanics — guild wars, territory control, and coordinated raid encounters against the Navy.
